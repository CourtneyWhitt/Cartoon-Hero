Storm's Origin

One of the original and most powerful X-Men, originally born as Ororo Monroe. Storm is the descendant of a long line of mystical Kenyan priestesses. She has long flowing white hair and blue eyes and is majestic! This goddess was also once married to T'challa from black panther. I like to call her mother nature.
